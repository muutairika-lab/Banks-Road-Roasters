Our Coffee

Every cup at Banks Road Roasters is made using our premium coffee beans and fresh coffee beans, roasted in small batches to bring out the rich, full flavour our Mt Pleasant customers love. We occasionally offer freshly baked treats from The Miller’s Bakery, a beloved local favourite. Prices are marked individually. Stop by and see if we have their famous fresh raisin bread — it sells out fast and pairs perfectly with our Banks Road Roasters coffee.
